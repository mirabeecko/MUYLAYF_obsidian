# inline CVIKY

Stretching: Important to stretch your groin (butterfly stretch), quads and hip flexor (stand up straight..bend leg to your butt, hold quad stretch and lean slightly forward), hamstrings (sit on ground..touch toes), Leg abductors...IE IT Band (lay on edge of bed, couch, sidewalk, etc..with your butt facing the edge..shift top leg back slightly and have partner steady your hips and gently push down on your top leg somewhere around your knee...should feel stretch in hip and outer leg), calf stretch, Tibialis anterior stretch (shin...point toe on ground, stretch forward). 

Exercises: Squats, lunges (hold dumbbells out in T formation, forcing your legs to balance side-to-side, as well as forward...also a killer shoulder workout), leg extensions, leg curls. Deadlifts would also be very useful. weighted crunches, oblique crunches (sides of abdominals), Roman Chair leg lifts for lower abs. Weighted hyper extensions for lower back. 

Also, check out a site on plyometrics. I believe these training drills would be very beneficial to inline skaters. All about explosive power and agility. 

Good luck, and for God's sake wear a helmet...crazy kids.

# Expert product launch tips in the new net magazine

Discover Rachel Andrew's top tips on pricing, support, marketing and much more in the latest issue of net magazine, on sale now!

![[./_resources/Expert_product_launch_tips_in_the_new_net_magazine__Web_design__Creative_Bloq.resources/net251cover535.jpg]]

"If I had one top tip for other people who want to launch a product it is to start small. Launch with the smallest possible thing that you can get people to pay you money for." These wise words come from Rachel Andrew, who in this issue shares her advice on building a business model around your idea and working out an infrastructure for delivering the product and supporting your customers.

Elsewhere in the magazine we look at the design foundations that fuelled Google Maps' latest evolution, and there are tutorials on using the Gumby CSS framework to build slick responsive sites, creating apps with Node.js, mastering SVG filter effects and much more.

Our main interview this month is with digital artist Seb Lee-Delisle, and top of all that, there's also the usual mix of inspirational new work, practical advice, informed analysis and thought-provoking comment.

So, what are you waiting for? Get the latest issue of net magazine now in your favourite format!
